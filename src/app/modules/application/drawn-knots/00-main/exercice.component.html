let's draw knots

the knots, here objects, method and instruments of investigation without a priori, for themselves, are those that are said to be primary, reduced to their smallest number of intersections. their metamorphoses are drawn and studied, forming clusters, magmas and clouds. they are continuous, sliding and tilting, regular and irregular, real or virtual. they are the reversals, the permutations and others, the transversions and still other transitions. the plasticity of rare nodes forms the restriction as is said to be general that of nodeian clouds.
